# Rijal Source Books — Download Catalog

Books of biographical criticism (جرح وتعديل), tabaqat, and biographical dictionaries used by Mir Hamid Husain in Abaqat al-Anwar to build his ترجمه dossiers. These are the books needed for cross-referencing narrator evaluations in the data model.

Hadith collections, tafsir, tarikh, and kalam works are **excluded** — those are the *content* being defended and will number in the hundreds.

**Note:** This is a Gemini-extracted minimum. A manual pass through the raw volumes will expand this list. Books #56–61 were found in the Arabic dossier text (Vol 3) but not captured in the per-volume Gemini summaries.

### The Tahdhib Chain

One key pattern: the rijal books form an intellectual chain that Mir Hamid Husain exploits:

```
Al-Kamal (Abd al-Ghani al-Maqdisi, d.600)
  → Tahdhib al-Kamal (al-Mizzi, d.742) — expanded it
    → Tadhhib al-Tahdhib (al-Dhahabi, d.748) — abridged it
      → Khulasah (al-Khazraji, d.923) — further abridged
    → Tahdhib al-Tahdhib (Ibn Hajar, d.852) — re-expanded from Mizzi
      → Taqrib al-Tahdhib (Ibn Hajar) — condensed his own Tahdhib
```

By citing multiple books in this chain, the author shows the same narrator evaluation is consistent across centuries of Sunni scholarship.

#2 📜 Introduction
Author's Introduction — Ibn Taymiyyah's Denial & Scholar Catalog
149 lines · L259–407
▶

The introduction outlines the author's plan to authenticate Hadith al-Safinah despite attacks from its enemies, clarify its implication of the Imamate and caliphate of the Ahl al-Bayt, and refute the absurd claims of the addressee, particularly those of Dehlavi. It highlights Ibn Taymiyyah's denial of the hadith from Minhaj al-Sunnah and counters it by listing numerous prominent scholars from early and later periods who narrated it with multiple chains. The author emphasizes the hadith's widespread transmission to silence opponents and strengthen faith.

Hadith Text
مثل أهلبيتى مثل سفيئة نوح
Ibn Taymiyyah
As for his saying: 'The likeness of my Ahl al-Bayt is like the ark of Noah'—this has no known chain of narration at all; neither Sahih nor Hasan nor even weak in the books of hadith upon which reliance is placed. Even if it were narrated, it would be by night talkers who narrate fabrications; this only adds weakness and repulsiveness to it.
Verbatim

Source

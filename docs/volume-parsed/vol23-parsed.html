#2 📜 Introduction
Author's Introduction
4 lines • L259–262
▶

In this preface (tamhid), Mir Hamid Husain addresses an opponent's demand to substantiate the Hadith al-Safinah in relation to an upcoming statement, amid clear hostility and aggression from the Ahl al-Sunnah scholars. He announces his plan for the volume: first, to prove and establish the authenticity of Hadith al-Safinah despite the denial and enmity of some Sahabah; second, to clarify and explain it under its own dedicated chapter. This sets the stage for a robust defense against Sunni objections.
